\subsection{Matt Smith: a great Doctor, or the greatest Doctor? Bonus: What about Jodie?}
To preface this, I have never seen an episode of Doctor Who in my life. That being said, I have seen clips and heard opinions from my friends in secondary (British system for middle + high school). And from what I have seen, Matt Smith is a great Doctor but I gotta say that the greatest Doctor has to be David Tennant. Both from how I seen the actors play the character, and from the other pieces of media I've seen them in.

As for Jodie, I have not seen enough from her to formulate an opinion nor am I involved enough in the Doctor Who fandom to decide whether it is a good Doctor. I am aware that her character was controversial for being the first female Doctor, but as long as her character had meaningful writing and development outside of just that then I don't see the issue.
